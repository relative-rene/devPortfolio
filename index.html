About me

To Whom It May Concern, Looking for a creative, self starter who has a passion for web design? Call off the search, because I am ready for the job. After recently completing General Assembly’s Web Development immersive course, I have a proven ability to learn a new technology and contribute functional transparent code. General Assembly’s WDI is a 12-week intensive course focused on working with databases such as Postgres, MySQL ,MongoDB, and languages/frameworks such as JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, Angular, Node.js and Express. After 1000+ hours of structured and unstructured learning, I have built web based applications from wireframes to deployment via Heroku. Cutting my teeth in an Agile environment, I am comfortable seeking answers, failing quickly and contributing tested code with a Git workflow. I bring a Bachelor's Degree in Criminal Justice and Minor in Holistic Health. Before pursuing my interest in Web Development, these credential helped me as a Family Case Manager at Glide’s Walk In Center. My day to day consisted of family assessments, creating strength based success plans and empower the client to self sufficiency. At any point, I had over 50+ families that received assistance applying for benefits, Domestic Violence Safety plans, eviction notices, CPS cases ,employment assistance and parenting workshops. Despite having different issues and coming from all walks of life, I always knew that dramatic life changes come from focusing opportunities. Regularly, I would emphasis to my clients that opportunity looks a lot like hard work. From a field worker to a Case Manager, I never had a job I was better than, just an opportunity.

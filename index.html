About me

To Whom It May Concern, Looking for a creative, self starter who has a passion for web design? Call off the search, because I am ready for the job. After recently completing General Assembly’s Web Development immersive course, I have a proven ability to learn a new technology and contribute functional transparent code. General Assembly’s WDI is a 12-week intensive course focused on working with databases such as Postgres, MySQL ,MongoDB, and languages/frameworks such as JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, Angular, Node.js and Express. After 1000+ hours of structured and unstructured learning, I have built web based applications from wireframes to deployment via Heroku. Cutting my teeth in an Agile environment, I am comfortable seeking answers, failing quickly and contributing tested code with a Git workflow. I bring a Bachelor's Degree in Criminal Justice and Minor in Holistic Health. Despite having different issues and coming from all walks of life, I always knew that dramatic life changes come from focusing opportunities.
